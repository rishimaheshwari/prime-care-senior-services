Why we were founded

Care built on decades of healthcare experience

After decades in healthcare, Dr. Maheshwari recognized that many seniors do not need hospitalization or institutional care — they simply need reliable support, companionship, and assistance with daily living.

That realization inspired Prime Care Senior Services LLC. Our mission is to help seniors age with dignity and independence through compassionate, dependable, and personalized non-medical home care throughout Northeast Ohio. While we do not practice medicine or provide medical treatment, our leadership’s extensive healthcare background helps us better understand the needs and challenges facing seniors and their families.
